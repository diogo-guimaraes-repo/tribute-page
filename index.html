John Petrucci

A Tribute to a Prog Legend

Some Cool John Petrucci Facts

  • Started playing guitar to got to bed later
  • Went to Berkeley Music School with Mike Pornoy and John Myung
  • Formed Dream Theater
  • One of the best guitar players in the world
  • Member of Liquid Tension Experiment
  • Has made 2 solo albums
  • Sometimes he melts the face of the front row in concerts
  • Has toured with G3 multiple times
  • An incredible human being
  • Has made a custom beard oil called Nebula
When I think of a lot of the players I admire, they could always play their parts without hiding behind distortion and sustain. Put the time in. Hear your mistakes. Yeah, it sucks, it's humbling, it makes you want to throw the guitar out the window. But if you work on your mistakes, they'll eventually go away, and you'll become a strong player.
